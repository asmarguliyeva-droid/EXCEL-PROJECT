# ☕ Coffee Sales Analysis Dashboard (Ukraine 2024–2025)

## 📊 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Excel dashboard analyzing coffee sales data across Ukraine for the years 2024 and 2025. The dashboard provides valuable business insights into sales performance, customer demand, seasonal trends, pricing dynamics, and customer behavior.

The goal of this analysis is to help stakeholders identify growth opportunities, understand market trends, and make data-driven decisions regarding product pricing, inventory management, and customer relationships.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall coffee sales performance
* Identify top-performing coffee products
* Monitor seasonal demand patterns
* Track price fluctuations across coffee varieties
* Evaluate customer purchasing behavior
* Compare sales trends between 2024 and 2025

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### Sales Performance

* Total sales revenue
* Sales trends over time
* Year-over-year comparison

### Customer Analysis

* Top clients by sales value
* Customer contribution to revenue
* Purchasing patterns

### Demand Analysis

* Product demand by coffee type
* Best-selling coffee varieties
* Market preferences

### Seasonal Trends

* Monthly and quarterly sales analysis
* Peak and low-demand periods
* Seasonal purchasing behavior

### Price Analysis

* Coffee price fluctuations
* Comparison of pricing trends between 2024 and 2025
* Impact of price changes on sales performance
